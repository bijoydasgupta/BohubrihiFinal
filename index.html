About

BIJOY DAS GUPTA

Student, Learner & YouTuber

I’m from a middle-class family of Dhaka, I am Bijoy Das Gupta. Nobody comes in this world, without the support of family and friends. Actually, whatever you will be, it is just because of your family. My father is a respectable businessman in our community. My mother is a housewife. They both love their occupation. That’s I have learned from my parents the value of time, honesty, hard work and commitment to the purpose. Then I have an elder brother.

I am a keen reader of novels and history books as I have a strong interest in Japanese Anime and classical architecture. I love to read sci-fi stories, mystery-solving stories, and romantic stories. At my pre-childhood, I used to listen to stories from my grandmother, and this has a long-lasting effect on me.

Everybody has an ambition in life. No man can do anything in the world without aim. So, I want to be a hunter who is always hunting for his dream. And this dream must be mixed with hard work & sincerity.
